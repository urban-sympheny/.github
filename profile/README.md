## About Us
At Sympheny, we believe the energy transition is a global challenge that requires locally adaptable solutions. Our mission is to provide software that is globally scalable, helping facility managers and site owners navigate the emerging energy landscape with precision and insight.

## Our Open Source Ecosystem
This organization is the home for tools designed to help you integrate Sympheny’s intelligence into your own workflows. Here you will find:

* **SDKs:** Official libraries to interact with our SaaS API.
* **Integration Tools:** Scripts and plugins to connect Sympheny with your existing tech stack.
* **Examples & Templates:** Reference implementations for digital twin modeling and energy optimization.
